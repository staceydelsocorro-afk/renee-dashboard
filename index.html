3. Viral Pattern Identification

5 shorts / week is the growth floor
Same cadence Vinh Giang ran to reach 4.49M subs. Renée's matching this — and seeing the +49% YouTube lift to prove it. Maintain. Do not reduce.
Communication-skills niche beats "presence/aesthetic"
Renée and Vinh, both anchored in communication, are at +49%. Vanessa drifted into body language and dropped to -31%. The audience signal favors skill-based framing over personality framing.
Shorts-only ceiling exists at ~1.2M subs
Vanessa runs 7 shorts/wk + barely any longform → -31% view decline. The ceiling is real. Longform is what breaks it.
"Share in the comments" + 3-item lists are the failure pattern
Explicitly flagged in the May 30 Glamour Grit revision: cut 3-item lists, replace "share in the comments" with one specific, answerable question.
Platform breadth is a strength — depth is the gap
Platform diversity scored 8/10 (highest of any metric). Don't add a sixth channel — go deeper on YouTube and LinkedIn.
